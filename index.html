Welcome to Niftons Arena!

In this real-time strategy multiplayer game, you build paths for your little Niftons and lead them through the arena. You have 30 minutes, but it's not gonna be easy! Be ready to cross your paths with hundreds of enemy Niftons and get in some serious trouble. Good news, you can fight other Niftons with deadly weapons and suprise them with unexpected magic. This game is all about strategy and skill. Only the best player can lead the most Niftons through the arena and win the game!
