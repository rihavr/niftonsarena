Welcome to Niftons Arena!

Step into the multiplayer action of Niftons Arena, an intensely competitive strategy game. Guide your Niftons safely across the arena through chaotic challenges and battles. This game of skill demands quick thinking and prompt reactions, with enemy Niftons always trying to disrupt your perfect plans. Compete against friends or other players, use weapons and magic to turn the game in your favor, and enjoy an endlessly replayable experience with unique situations and strategies in each match.
